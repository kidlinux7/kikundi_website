About Us

Kikundi is a digital platform for saving, accessing and managing credit, and investing through saving groups and small financial institutions (MFIs) and Angel networks.

The purpose of Kikundi is to unlocking saving groups as a funding source for East African social entrepreneurs by providing them with simple and friendly financial product and services while looking for capital for their new or existing business.
