Re-imagining Financial Health for youths.


Building friendly digital tools to enable youths to save, invest and build a financial health using saving group methodologies, AI, machine learning, data and 4iR technology.

Easy group management

Instant transfers

User cash back rewards

Track your credit score

Bank-grade security

Real-time notifications

Our mission

To help social entrepreneurs in East Africa to build a good financial health, access more funding to grow their businesses faster.

Our Vission

To be a solution that positively redefines youth's relationship with money and enables them to build wealth using modern technology.
